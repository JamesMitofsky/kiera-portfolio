Welcome to my page! I’m a Canadian artist living in the English Channel on the Isle of Jersey. If you’re looking for an extroverted cartoonist, illustrator, and animator to crack some jokes and make some art, you’ve come to the right place.

I’m an honours graduate of Arts University Bournemouth with a degree in Animation Production. I have been working professionally as an artist since 2018, as both a freelance artist and an in-house cartoonist for Jersey’s Kartoon Faktory. My work has been exhibited in the Jersey Museum, the CCA Gallery of St Helier, as well as having been published in several children’s books. I have worked as a character designer, storyboard artist, animator, visual development artist, cartoonist, illustrator, director, and writer throughout my freelance and university work.

I have a taste for working traditionally, however I am more than proficient in industry standard software, including but not limited to: Photoshop, After Effects, Premier Pro, ToonBoom Harmony, and Procreate. I'm an enthusiastic worker and team player, and I’m always keen to learn new things and take on any challenges that come with the job. I love creating art and any new way to do that is welcome knowledge to me.
